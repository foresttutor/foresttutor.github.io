About

Math did not come naturally to me. I've developed my skills with the support of others, rigorous training, and thousands of hours of instruction and practice. I empathize when a student does not know where to start when faced with a word problem or when concepts are just not clicking.

A teacher's instruction style or the classroom structure may not always be accessible to every student's learning style or executive functioning. Working with a patient and supportive tutor who can answer questions in real time encourages students' growth, self-confidence, and skill development.

I'm Forest, an Incoming 8th Grade Math Teacher with experience supporting students as a tutor and educator since 2017. I would love to connect to discuss your students' needs and goals as well as how I can support them!
